About Me

Hi, I'm Jason. I'm a Junior Data Engineer with experience designing automated data pipelines, building data validation systems, and developing scalable data workflows in Python, SQL, AWS, and PySpark. I recently completed an internship at ASE US Inc., where I designed and implemented ETL and reporting pipelines to automate quotation pricing data processing, generate user-facing reports, and maintain reliable long-term backups in the company database for pricing analytics in the semiconductor industry. I hold an M.S. in Computational Media from UCSC, where my masters thesis focused on applying AI and machine learning to automated testing of 3D video games in Unreal Engine 5. I have built AI-driven testing tools and player analytics pipelines to dive into in-game behavior, find bugs, and help games run more smoothly. I’m always curious about the ways data can tell stories — whether it’s analyzing player behavior in a game or uncovering insights from business data. I enjoy blending technical skills with creative problem-solving across everything I work on.
